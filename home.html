About Beautified Hair Salon

Beautified Hair Lounge consistently delivers precision haircuts and
expert color services to our eclectic clientele. Our unique collective of talent bring their own skills and specialties, making our
work creative, exciting and individually tailored to each client. As an artistic expression hair grows and evolves with the times,
which is why Voodoo and our stylists have made a commitment to our clientele to keep up!

Cuts & Color

Cuts – Classic to Club, we are obsessed with shape and
texture. Sit with a cutting expert and let us help you
tailor a look that suits your face shape and highlights
your most striking attributes.
Color - Natural sun-kissed hand-painted highlights, funky
fashion colors, depth, dimension, cool, warm…
oh my! Let our world class color team have a
crack at matching your skin tone and personality
with the perfect complimentary hair color.

Why Us?
